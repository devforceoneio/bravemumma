About

Bravemumma is the 'birth of a brave new era'. It has been born from the silent pain and suffering endured every day, following a traumatic birth. The book The Day My Vagina Broke was written to break the silence on such a taboo topic and help advocate for change in this childbirth space. What we think of birth, what we say about birth and how we treat one another about childbirth. The main aim is to ensure women can feel empowered to make informed decisions about their birth – and beyond in motherhood. Bravemumma aims to support this change.

The Author

First and foremost, Stephanie is an awesome and brave mumma (and wife). The long journey to becoming a mum is her greatest achievement. Her ‘never-die’ attitude and resilience to adversity was never going to let cancer or birth trauma stop her from trying to be the ‘best’ mum she can be. Stephanie lives with her loving husband, two little people and two fat cats on the South Coast of New South Wales. When she manages to find a spare 32 minutes, she enjoys watching the ABC's The Let Down, while folding the Mt.Washmore of baby clothes with a cup of tea.
